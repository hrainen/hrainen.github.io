About me

I recently graduated with a B.S. in Computer Science from the University of Nothern Arizona. I interned at the NASA Jet Propulsion Laboratory during the summer of 2017. I am excited to work at a company designing and implementing ideas on the cutting edge of technology.

While I love solving hard problems, I also enjoy optimizing the performance of existing systems to get them to go as fast as they can go. There is something extremely satisfying when you get a program to run 10 times as fast as before.
