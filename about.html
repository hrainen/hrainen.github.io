About me

I recently graduated with a B.S. in Computer Science from Northern Arizona University. I interned at the NASA Jet Propulsion Laboratory during the summer of 2017. During my time there, I found a passion for designing and working on cutting edge technology. I love experimenting and discovering new ideas and spend a fair amount of time looking at programming news and cool ideas.

While I love solving new and hard problems, I also enjoy optimizing the performance of existing systems to get them to go as fast as they can go. There is something extremely satisfying when you get a program to run 10 times as fast as before. Whether I am working on existing software or on the bleeding edge I am excited to leave my mark on the software industry.

Interests
